Alice Lim

Summary

Alice Lim (she/her) is a 22-year-old computer scientist and software engineer. She was born in Indonesia into a Hakka Chinese family, but now lives and works in Hong Kong.

She currently serves as Distinguished Engineer at ParallelChain Lab. In this role, she created ParallelChain F--a high-performance blockchain smart contract platform--and HotStuff-rs, a Rust implementation of the HotStuff consensus protocol. She remains as maintainer of both projects, and leads ParallelChain Lab's public blockchain R&D efforts.
