Bio

Alice Lim is a computer scientist and software engineer. She was born in Indonesia into a Hakka Chinese family, but now lives and works in Hong Kong.

She is currently distinguished engineer at ParallelChain Lab. In this role, she created HotStuff-rs, a Rust implementation of the HotStuff consensus protocol, and ParallelChain F--a high-performance blockchain smart contract platform. She remains as maintainer of both projects, and plays a leading role in ParallelChain Lab's public blockchain R&D efforts.
